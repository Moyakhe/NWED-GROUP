For The Ballers

Our Story

Welcome to "For The Ballers", where we blend traditional flavours with a modern twist. We are located in Cape Town, with our main restaurant based in Italy. We serve a variety of food such as seafood, breakfast, dessert, vegan food and most refreshing drinks. Our restaurant is Halaal and is all inclusive. All our ingredients are locally sourced and freshly picked. Our employees are specially trained and offer warm, welcoming dining experience.As a high class restaurant, we hope to not only showcase our quality through our aesthetic but also through our flavours and presentation. As our restaurant continues to grow, we hope to evolve in many ways as well as intergrate ourselves further into the community, After all, without the community there would be no us.

Founder History

For The Ballers started as a small family business in Milan, Italy. It was founded by Gabriele Bianco in 2010. Bianco was born into a family of 14, as the eldest child. His father died when he was 16, as a result, he had to fill the void in the house left by his late father. He worked odd jobs throughout his highschool career while maintaining impressive academic performance He went to culinary school at the age of 22. However he had to drop out due to financial constraints. It was then that he was inspired to start his own restarant. With his passion for cooking, the restaurant soon became a success.

Our Chefs

We have a bounty of professionally trained chefs from all over the world, each from various age groups contributing talent and wisdom on the plate. At our restaurant we don't believe in age instead, believe in the passion one has for cooking. So whether you're fresh out of culinary school or have decades of experience, so long as you have a burning passion for cooking, you're welcome at our restaurant. At FOR THE BALLERS we dont just serve the community, we serve our members and provide them with a nurturing environment, experience and skills necessary for them to grow to their greatest versions of themselves. Our testimony includes Chef Gladys, who is now a two star Michelin chef and is an expert in Italian cuisine with an added french twist. She is also one of our three head chefs, alongside chef Alonso and chef Thabang. These three chefs are part of our story and part of the foundation of "For The Ballers".
