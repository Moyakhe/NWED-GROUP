Breakfast Recipe

Fluffy Lemon Ricotta Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powdwer
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Whisk ricotta, eggs, milk, and lemon zest until smooth.
  2. In another bowl, combine flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  3. Fold dry ingredients into the ricotta mixture gently.
  4. Heat butter in a pan and cook pancakes until golden on both sides.
  5. Serve warm with honey or fresh berries.

Seafood Recipe

Shrimp Linguine With Wine Sauce

Ingredients

  • 400g linguine pasta
  • 500g Large shrimp peeled and deveined
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 cup vegetable or seafood stock
  • 2 tbsp salted butter
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper,to taste
  • Fresh parsley,chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook linguine in salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan. Add garlic and saute until fragrant.
  3. Add shrimp, season lightly, and cook until pink and just firm.
  4. Pour in white wine and simmer for 2-3 minutes to reduce slightly.
  5. Add stock and butter, stirring until the sauce becomes silky.
  6. Return linguine to the pan, toss with sauce and shrimp, and finish with lemon juice.
  7. Season to taste, garnish with parsley, and serve immediately.

Indian Recipe

Prawn Curry with Coconut Cream

Ingredients

  • 500g prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic,minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp curry powder (or a blend of turmeric, cumin, coriander)
  • 1 tsp chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 Can (400ml) coconut cream
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes (fresh or canned)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, for garnish
  • Steamed basmati rice, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan.Add onion and saute until golden.
  2. Stir in garlic and ginger, cooking until aromatic
  3. Add curry powder and chili powder, toasting briefly to release flavor
  4. Mix in tomatoes and simmer until they soften into a sauce.
  5. Pour in coconut cream, stir well, and bring to a gentle simmer.
  6. Add prawns, cook for 5-7 minutes until pink and tender.
  7. Season to taste, garnish with fresh coriander, and serve hot with basmati rice

Vegetarian Recipe

Roasted Cauliflower Steak

Ingredients

  • 1 large head of cauliflower
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley or microgreens, for garnish
  • Lemon wedges, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Remove leaves and trim the stem of the cauliflower, keeping the core intact.
  3. Slice cauliflower into thick "steaks" (about 2-3 cm each).
  4. Mix olive oil, garlic, paprika, cumin, salt,and pepper in a small bowl.
  5. Brush both sides of the steaks with the seasoned oil mixture.
  6. Place on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and tender.
  7. Garnish with parsley or microgreens, and serve with lemon wedges

Dessert Recipe

German Apple Pancake

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 medium apples, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • Powdered sugar, for dusting

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. In a bowl, whisk eggs, flour, milk, granulated sugar, vanilla, and salt until smooth
  3. In an oven-safe skillet, melt butter,Add apple slices, brown sugar, and cinnamon; saute until apples soften and caramelize
  4. Cook for about 3-5 minutes until the apples soften and the sugar turns into a light syrup.
  5. Now carefully remove the hot skillet from the oven, add the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter, and swirl it around until melted.
  6. Spread the caramelized apples evenly in the hot pan and pour the butter over them,and immediately return the skillet to the oven.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, until the pancake puffs up dramatically around the edges and turns golden brown.
  8. Serve right away while hot and fluffy.
